HTML5 Elements You Need

1. <dialog> - Native Modal Windows

Create modal windows without JavaScript frameworks.

This is a Dialog

No JavaScript frameworks required!

 
<dialog id="myDialog">
  <h3>Dialog Title</h3>
  <button onclick="this.closest('dialog').close()">Close</button>
</dialog>
<button onclick="document.getElementById('myDialog').showModal()">Open</button>

2. <details> & <summary> - Collapsible Sections

Expandable/collapsible content without JavaScript.

Click to expand

This content can be expanded and collapsed without any JavaScript!

<details>
  <summary>Click to expand</summary>
  <p>Hidden content here</p>
</details>

3. <datalist> - Autocomplete for Inputs

Native autocomplete suggestions.

<input list="browsers">
<datalist id="browsers">
  <option value="Chrome">
  <option value="Firefox">
</datalist>

4. <meter> - Visual Value Gauges

Display measurements within a range.

75%
85% 
<meter value="75" min="0" max="100" low="30" high="70" optimum="0">75%</meter>

5. <progress> - Progress Bars

Show completion progress.

70% 
<progress value="70" max="100">70%</progress>

6. <output> - Dynamic Calculation Results

Display calculation results with semantic meaning.

+ = 15 
<form oninput="result.value = parseInt(a.value) + parseInt(b.value)">
  <input type="number" id="a" value="5"> +
  <input type="number" id="b" value="10"> =
  <output name="result" for="a b">15</output>
</form>

7. <mark> - Highlighted Text

Semantic highlighting for search results or emphasis.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

<p>The <mark>quick brown fox</mark> jumps over the lazy dog.</p>

8. <time> - Dates and Times

Machine-readable dates with human-friendly display.

Article published: May 20, 2025

Event starts: 2:30 PM on May 20, 2025

Store opens: 9:00 AM

Flight time: 2 hours and 30 minutes

<!-- Just a date -->
<time datetime="2025-05-20">May 20, 2025</time>

<!-- Date and time -->
<time datetime="2025-05-20T14:30:00">2:30 PM on May 20, 2025</time>

<!-- Just a time -->
<time datetime="14:30:00">2:30 PM</time>

<!-- A duration -->
<time datetime="PT2H30M">2 hours and 30 minutes</time>

9. <figure> & <figcaption> - Captioned Content

Self-contained content with captions.

<figure>
  <img src="chart.jpg" alt="Sales chart">
  <figcaption>Q2 2025 Sales Performance</figcaption>
</figure>
